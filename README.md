# Task-3-Deep-Dive-Analysis-Interactive-Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow, from raw data preparation to business intelligence reporting. The dataset was cleaned using Python and analyzed using Power BI to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and category-wise revenue insights.

The final deliverable is an interactive dashboard that enables users to explore key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

## Project Objectives

* Clean and prepare raw sales data for analysis.
* Perform exploratory data analysis to identify business trends.
* Analyze sales performance across products, categories, and customer segments.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

## Tools & Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Power BI Desktop
* Microsoft Excel

## Data Cleaning Process

The raw dataset was cleaned using Python by:

* Handling missing values.
* Removing duplicate records.
* Standardizing data formats.
* Converting date fields to proper datetime format.
* Creating additional time-based fields such as Year, Month, and Quarter.
* Preparing the dataset for business intelligence analysis.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Sales

### Visualizations

* Sales by Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top Products by Sales
* Sales Distribution by Gender

### Interactive Filters

* Year
* Category
* City

## Key Business Insights

### Category Performance

Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue, indicating strong customer demand and greater business contribution.

### Product Analysis

A small group of products contributed a major portion of overall sales, highlighting opportunities for focused marketing and inventory planning.

### Sales Trends

Monthly sales patterns provide insights into business performance over time and can support forecasting and strategic planning.

### Customer Insights

Customer purchasing behavior varied across demographic groups, offering opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus promotional efforts on high-performing product categories.
* Maintain sufficient inventory for top-selling products.
* Use customer segmentation to improve marketing effectiveness.
* Monitor monthly sales trends to support forecasting and planning.
* Leverage dashboard insights for data-driven decision-making.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the complete analytics lifecycle, including data preparation, business analysis, dashboard development, and insight generation. The interactive Power BI dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business intelligence that can support strategic decision-making.
